Programs

Four Programs. One Curriculum. Built for Schools and Colleges.

Every program teaches the same core skill: how to build AI, not just use it. Pick the entry point that fits your institution - student-led upskilling, teacher capacity-building, full lab deployment, or a portable AI kit for a single batch.

Program 01

Student AI Program

A structured, hands-on program for students in classes 9–12 and undergraduate years. Students do not just learn what AI is - they build, train, and ship a working AI project on real hardware. Cohort-based, mentor-led, and project-graded.

By the end, every student has a portfolio piece they understand deeply: dataset, model, training process, evaluation, and deployment. This is the project they will talk about in college applications and job interviews.

ForStudents in classes 9–12 and undergraduates (any stream with basic computer literacy)
Format12-week cohort · weekend hands-on sessions · GPU lab access · final project review
What they buildAn end-to-end AI project: data collection, model training, evaluation, deployment to an edge device
OutcomeWorking project, code on GitHub, certificate, mentor letter for college / scholarship applications
Batch size15–20 students per cohort (kept small on purpose)
FeePer-student fee · institutional discounts available · scholarships for merit-based admissions

Program 02

Teacher Training

A 5-day intensive that turns existing computer science, mathematics, and STEM teachers into confident AI educators. Teachers leave with a working classroom curriculum, hands-on hardware experience, and the support to run AI projects with their own students.

5-Day Workshop Structure

Day 1

Foundations

What AI actually is, what a neural network does, where it succeeds and fails.

Day 2

Datasets & Tools

Hands-on with Python, datasets, labelling pipelines, and the workflow of a real ML project.

Day 3

Training Models

Train a model end-to-end on GPU hardware. Evaluate, debug, improve.

Day 4

Edge Deployment

Run inference on edge devices. Understand performance, power, and real-world constraints.

Day 5

Classroom Playbook

Build your own lesson plans. Co-design a project your students can ship in a term.

ForSchool and college teachers in CS, mathematics, physics, or STEM-adjacent subjects
Format5-day intensive · on-campus or at our Lucknow lab · ongoing email and WhatsApp support after
What teachers leave withWorking AI projects of their own, classroom-ready curriculum, lesson plans, project rubrics
OutcomeCertified Edge AI Educator · ready to run student AI projects in their own classrooms
Batch size10–15 teachers per workshop
FeePer-teacher fee · institutional discounts for groups of 5+ from a single school or college

AI Student Kit

A portable AI lab in a box - for institutions that want to start small, run a single cohort, or pilot before committing to a full lab. Everything a student needs to learn AI hands-on for an entire program, and they keep the kit at the end.

Edge AI Hardware

A compact AI development board capable of running real models - vision, audio, and sensor-driven projects.

Sensor & Camera Module

Curriculum Workbook
